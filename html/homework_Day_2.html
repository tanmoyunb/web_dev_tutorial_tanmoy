THE OLD SALT
            

        
        

                                                                        I was walking through the dockyard in a panic
                                                                            When I saw a metalot old and grey.
                                                                         He was carrying his kitbag and his hammock 
                                                                             And as he passed I heard him say,
                  
                                                                                "Oh I wonder, yes i wonder
                                                                              Has the Jaunty made a blunder
                                                                          When he made this draft chit out for me.
                                                                             For I've been a barrack stanchion
                                                                                 In good old Jago's Mansions
                                                                                And I don't want to go to sea!"
